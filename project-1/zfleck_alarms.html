Interested in alarms? Probably not, but I am!

Okay, so I have an interesting hobby to say the least! I LOVE to figure out how things work, and then hands on work on them myself. That is how I became interested in Fire Alarm technology of all things. What blows me away is just how technically advanced a thing most people find annoying (shout out to the students in MSV) can actually be, and not to mention how much your life can depend on it!

Back in middle school, I had a friend who's dad happened to own a fire alarm inspection company. I expressed my interest and he helped me get my hands on some devices to work with, to help me start tinkering. Shortly there after, I began collecting things of my own, and also began buying parts for my collection online. Soon enough, I found myself with a system up and running in my house.

With that home system, I began filming some videos to post on YouTube, in hopes I could help teach others about the world of fire alarm. To my surprise, those videos got attention and with 1600 subscribers and counting, I have some extra money from YouTube ads coming into my pocket every month. Pretty neat! Eventually this turned into a summer job for me, and I spend my summers inspecting and maintaining fire alarm systems for a local company.
